Sydney, Australia

Sydney is the largest and most popular city in Australia, located on the east coast of the country. It is the capital of the state New South Wales. Sydney is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Harbour Bridge, the Royal Botanic Garden, and the Opera House, which is UNESCO World Heritage Site. Sydney is surrounded by beautiful beaches, parks and museums. It is a popular destination for surfing, hiking, and climbing the Harbour Bridge.
